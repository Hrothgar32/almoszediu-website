Trains The State Of Not Being

Álmos Zediu  published on 2020-06-17   included in rants
Tis strange, but it is certainly true to some extent: being on trains is like being in another plane of existence: it is a separate social place, with ever changing, ever moving background. Because of the vast space it encompasses, it does not feel like a vehicle, it feels like another moving world. The Feeling of a New Start Every time I embark on a train trip, I feel like I’m going to start my life over, like a hopeful lad who tries he’s fortune in the New World.
